About

Hometown: Hershey, Pennsylvania
Age: 23
Enjoys: Long Walks on the Beach. Puppies/Kittens, Singing, Video Games, Venti Iced Soy Caramel Macchiatos


Bri Marie is a smooth R&B singer, whose aspirations took her from dreaming in her small hometown (Hershey, Pennsylvania) to actually reaching for those dreams in New York City. While her passion for singing developed in childhood, her love for songwriting blossomed just 3 years ago as it became a form of expression. Bri Marie combines her singing/songwriting talents with attention to detail in her debut EP 'Beginnings' to not only show the songstress' feelings in art form, but provide an enjoyable experience to listeners.


"Track after track on this project is raw, open, and emotional leaving us wanting to hear even more from the talented songstress."
- ThisIsRnB on 'Beginnings'
